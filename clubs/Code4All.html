Code4All

Information Technology

The Code4All Club is a student-led initiative that aims to promote coding skills and awareness among the youth. The club organizes workshops, hackathons, and various events to help students learn and develop their programming abilities. The club fosters a collaborative environment where members can explore different technologies, work on projects, and share knowledge, making coding accessible to all, regardless of their background or experience level.
